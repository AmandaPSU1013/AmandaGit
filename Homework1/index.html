About Me

Amanda Kramer(nee Smukler) Was born on October 13th, 1982 to Michael and Diane Smukler. In 2001, She graduated from Lower Moreland High School. After High school, she attended Penn State University, where she majored in Marketing. She graduated with honors in 2005. After a few years of working in sales, She was introduced to the mortgage industry. She has been in the industry for almost 10 years and works as an underwriter.

Amanda's Hobbies include Penn State Football, exploring the philadelphia food scene, working out, and spending time with her family and friends. She met her husband, Evan Kramer, in October 2012. They have spent the past 6 years traveling the world, going to as many sports events as possible, and eating at every restaurant in Center City. They were married on March 26th, 2017. Amanda resides in Center City Philadelphia
